Welcome

Welcome to the homepage of Our Lady of Guadalupe Parish. Thank you for taking the time to connect with us. Our parish was founded in 1931 by a small group of Mexican Immigrants and continues to thrive today with life and enthusiasm. We are proud to have shared this parish with Fr. Kevin Kenny for the past 10 years. On behalf of Our Lady of Guadalupe parish we invite you to look over our website and sign up for volunteer opportunities, special event e-blasts, or mass alerts. If you're ever in the neighborhood, stop by for a visit and say hello.

Faith Formation

The Faith Formation program at Our Lady of Guadalupe is a vital part of our thriving parish. In 2014 Our Lady of Guadalupe had a record number of 150 youth confirmed at the Cathedral in St. Paul. With numbers like this Our Lady of Guadalupe has an army of strong dedicated volunteers that help our youth grow and flourish in the Catholic faith.

Faith Formation Director Juana Mandujano (651­228­0506 ext. #12) (we will need to create an email for her)
Programs Available:

• First Reconciliation & Eucharist: One Year Program. Runs Wednesdays beginning September ­ April of each year (We should have a banner on this page that says “Registration for Fall Faith Formation begins 6/30/14 after each mass and during office hours)
