Welcome, To the homepage of Our Lady of Guadalupe Parish. Thank you for taking the time to connect with us. Our Parish was founded in 1931 by a small group of Mexican Immigrants and contunies to thrive today with life and enthusiam. We are proud to have shared this Parish with Fr. Kevin Kenny for the past 10 years. On behalf of Our Lady of Guadalupe Parish we invite you to browse our website and sign up for volunteer opportunties, special events (e-blast) or mass alerts. If you're ever in the neighborhood, stop by and say hello.
